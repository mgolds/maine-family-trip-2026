Plan

Morning

Easy checkout

  • No early alarms — the hard days are behind you.
  • Final sweep of the hotel room for chargers, stuffed animals, and swim gear.
  • Grab breakfast to-go if hitting the road early.
Optional

Hulls Cove Visitor Center

  • Quick stop at 25 Visitor Center Road for Junior Ranger badges, maps, or final souvenirs.
  • Nice farewell view if you skipped it earlier in the week.
  • Skip if schedule is tight — don't miss the flight.
Drive

Bar Harbor → Portland (~3 hours direct)

  • Take US-1 or I-295 depending on traffic and mood.
  • Gas up before the rental return — know the fuel policy.
  • Keep snacks and entertainment ready for the car ride.
Optional

One stop only — if ahead of schedule

  • Belfast: Waterfront walk and quick lunch — relaxed pace.
  • Camden: Revisit the harbor if you loved Day 3.
  • Rockland: Breakwater lighthouse walk — iconic but adds time.
  • Direct: Safest choice when flights are involved. Pick ONE or none.

Confirm before you go

Departure checklist

  • Flight time confirmed — work backward from airport arrival (2 hours domestic minimum).
  • Rental car return location and hours verified.
  • Gas tank filled to required level.
  • Car seats, bags, and carry-ons loaded before leaving the hotel.
  • Souvenirs packed in checked bags if needed (lobster-shaped anything is allowed!).
