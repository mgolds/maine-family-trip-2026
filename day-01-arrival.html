Plan

Arrival

Land & get settled

  • Collect luggage and head to ground transportation.
  • Confirm rental car pickup location and hours — some counters close late but have after-hours key drop/pickup.
  • Navigate to 209 Fore Street; hotel parking is available (confirm rate at check-in).
Check-in

Hampton Inn Portland Downtown-Waterfront

  • 209 Fore Street — waterfront location, walking distance to Old Port.
  • Unpack essentials only tonight; full Portland exploring starts tomorrow.
  • Confirm breakfast time for Day 2 — early ferry means an early start.
Optional

Old Port evening stroll

  • Walk Commercial Street and the waterfront if energy allows.
  • Listen for ferries and smell the salt air — welcome to Maine.
  • Skip restaurants tonight unless everyone is hungry; tomorrow is the food day.
