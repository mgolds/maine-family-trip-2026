Day 2

Old Port + Peaks Island + Eastern Promenade

Saturday, August 1, 2026 Sleep: Portland

Timing

Time Activity
8:00 AM Breakfast at hotel
8:30 AM Morning Old Port walk (no driving needed from hotel)
9:45 AM Walk to Casco Bay Lines ferry terminal
10:15 AM Ferry to Peaks Island (17-min crossing) — backup boats at 9:15 or 11:15
10:45 AM – 3:30 PM Peaks Island golf cart loop
4:00 PM Return ferry to Portland
5:30 PM Rest at hotel / early dinner
7:30 PM Eastern Promenade sunset walk

Parking

Where to leave the car

  • Casco Bay Lines: 56 Commercial Street — walk from hotel; no car needed for ferry day.
  • If driving: Ocean Gateway garage or Fore Street garage are closest downtown options.

Plan

Morning

Old Port on foot

  • Stroll Commercial Street, Exchange Street, and the Old Port waterfront — no car required from Fore Street.
  • Grab coffee and pastries; browse shops before the island adventure.
  • Watch the working waterfront — lobsters, ferries, and fishing boats.
Midday

Peaks Island ferry & golf cart loop

  • Catch the 10:15 AM ferry from Casco Bay Lines (alternatives: 9:15 or 11:15 AM).
  • Pick up reserved golf cart near the Peaks Island ferry landing.
  • Backshore: Rocky coastline and open ocean views on the island's far side.
  • Battery Steele: Historic WWII fort — fun for curious kids.
  • Beaches & tide pools: Explore at low tide; bring water shoes.
  • Ice cream stop: Reward the crew before the return ferry.
Evening

Eastern Promenade sunset

  • Drive or walk to Eastern Promenade Park for harbor views back toward the islands.
  • Wide paths perfect for scooters, strollers, or a slow family walk.
  • Watch the sky turn gold over Casco Bay — a gentle end to an active day.

  • Golf cart reservation: Confirm pickup time and location on the island.
