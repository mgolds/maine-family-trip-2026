Day 3

Portland → Camden → Mount Battie → Bar Harbor

Sunday, August 2, 2026 Sleep: Bar Harbor

Timing

Time Activity
8:00 AM Breakfast at Hampton Inn
8:45 AM Final Old Port walk / checkout prep
9:30 AM Leave Portland (US-1 North)
12:00 PM Arrive Camden — harbor lunch & stroll
1:30 PM Drive Mount Battie Road to summit
2:30 PM Continue north toward Bar Harbor
5:00 PM Check in — Atlantic Oceanside Hotel
Evening Pool swim, groceries, early bedtime for Cadillac sunrise

Important

Mount Battie Road closes at 4:00 PM

Plan to reach the Camden Hills State Park auto road summit lot well before 4 PM. If running late, skip the drive and enjoy Camden harbor instead.

Parking

Mount Battie summit

Camden Hills State Park auto road — pay at the gate. Summit parking lot fills on summer afternoons; go straight there after a quick lunch if time is tight.

Plan

Morning

Leave Portland

  • Checkout Hampton Inn, load the car, and head north on US-1.
  • Scenic coastal route — allow extra time for photo stops.
  • Optional Belfast stop only if kids need a break (playground, snack, stretch).
Midday

Camden Harbor

  • One of Maine's prettiest harbors — schooners, hills, and village shops.
  • Grab lunch near the waterfront.
  • Quick stroll along the harbor walk before heading up the mountain.
Afternoon

Mount Battie summit

  • Drive the auto road to the top for panoramic views of Penobscot Bay.
  • Short summit walk if time allows — see trail card below.
  • On a clear day you can see Acadia's peaks in the distance.
Evening

Bar Harbor arrival

  • Check in at Atlantic Oceanside Hotel — home base for five nights.
  • Let kids swim; adults stock up on groceries and snacks.
  • Early bedtime — tomorrow's Cadillac sunrise means a 3:30 AM wake-up.

Trails

Mount Battie Trail

Moderate ~1.1 mi

Forest hike alternative if you prefer walking over driving the auto road. Can combine with summit drive for a shorter walk at the top.

Confirm before you go

Cadillac sunrise prep (Day 4)

  • Cadillac Summit Road reservation: Verify your timed vehicle entry for tomorrow morning.
  • Acadia entrance pass: Have the pass ready — separate from the summit reservation.
  • Set alarms, lay out warm layers, and pack headlamps tonight.

Summit road gate times vary by season — confirm exact entry window on recreation.gov before leaving the hotel.
